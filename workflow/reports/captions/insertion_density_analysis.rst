Insertion density: initial vs. final
======================================

Per-gene insertion-site density at the initial and final timepoint, and their
comparison. The scatter panel colors genes by PomBase viability: essential
(inviable) genes should cluster below the y = x line (density drop) and at
negative log2 fold-change, since insertions disrupting them are lost over the
outgrowth period. Non-essential (viable) genes should cluster near y = x /
log2FC = 0. The gap between the two groups is the classic piggyBac
phenotyping signal.
